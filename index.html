We handle all types of DUI Matters

Underage DUI

If you are underage, and you are found to be drinking any amount of alcohol, you can face a DUI charge.

FELONY DUI

If you have three DUI charges within a 10 year period or if your DUI results in injury or death, it can be charged as a felony.

COMMERCIAL DUI

As a CDL driver you are held to a higher standard than that of a regularly licensed driver.

DUI DRUGS

These DUI cases involve allegations that impairment was caused not by alcohol, but by illegal or prescription medication.

Your Next Steps After a DUI Charge

01. Paperwork

Keep your tickets and any other paperwork or documents provided to you by the officer in a safe place and easily accessible.

02. safety

Get home safely. You may feel shaken and embarrassed, but it’s important to call someone you trust or use an Uber ride provided by the officer.

03. COURT DATE

Make your upcoming court date a top priority. This is a critical appearance which you will need to attend.

04. Contact us

The law requires that a DHSMV hearing is set within 10 days of a DUI arrest. In order to challenge the administrative suspension of your license we must act within the time limit or your license will receive an automatic suspension. Call us 24/7 or complete the free consultation form to get started on your defence.

05. HELP US HELP YOU

Share your story, provide any official documents and evidence, and let our experienced lawyers guide you through this challenging time.

06. STAY QUIET

Be mindful of who you talk to about this event. Keep your circle small and only share with those you trust.
